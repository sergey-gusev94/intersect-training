# How to make a nut butter sandwich

A simple and nutritious sandwich that takes just minutes to prepare. Perfect for a quick breakfast, lunch, or snack.

## Materials needed

### Base ingredients
- **Nut butter** (choose one)
  - Almond butter
  - Peanut butter
  - Cashew butter
- **Bread** (choose your preference)
  - White bread
  - Whole wheat bread
  - Multigrain bread

### Optional toppings
- Honey or jam
- Banana slices
- Granola
- Berries

## Process

1. **Slice the bread** - Cut to your preferred thickness (thicker slices are less likely to tear)
2. **Spread nut butter** - Apply an even layer of nut butter on one or both bread slices
3. **Add toppings** (optional) - Layer any additional ingredients like banana slices or honey
4. **Close sandwich** - Place slices together or serve open-faced
5. **Serve** - Enjoy immediately or wrap for later

## Tips

- **Prevent sogginess** - If using jam or honey, apply nut butter as a barrier first
- **Toasted option** - Lightly toast bread for better texture and flavor
- **Crust preference** - Remove crusts before serving if desired
- **Storage** - Best eaten fresh, but can be wrapped and stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 hours

## Serving suggestions

- Pair with fresh fruit and yogurt for a balanced meal
- Serve with a glass of milk or juice
- Makes 1 sandwich per 2 bread slices
