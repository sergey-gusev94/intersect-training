## How to Make a Nut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

You’ll need:

- 2 slices of bread
- Nut butter
- Jelly or jam
- A plate and butter knife

Instructions:

1. Place both slices of bread on the plate.
2. Spread nut butter evenly on one slice.
3. Wipe the knife clean, then spread jelly on the other slice.
4. Put the slices together with the spreads facing inward.
5. Cut the sandwich in half, if desired, and serve.

If nut allergies are a concern, use a nut-free alternative such as sunflower seed butter and avoid cross-contamination.
