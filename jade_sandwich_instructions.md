## How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

1. Take two pieces of bread and put them on a plate
2. Grab peanut butter from the peanut butter jar with a knife, spread on one piece of bread
3. Grab jelly from the jelly jar with a knife, spread on second piece of bread
4. Put pieces of bread together, forming a sandich
5. Enjoy delicious бутерброд
